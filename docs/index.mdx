Polymesh is an institutional-grade permissioned blockchain built specifically for regulated assets. It streamlines antiquated processes and opens the door to new financial instruments by solving challenges around governance, identity, compliance, confidentiality, and settlement.

## Why Polymesh?

Built from the ground up for regulated securities, Polymesh addresses the key challenges faced by financial institutions looking to issue, manage, and trade security tokens:

- **Identity and Compliance**: Built-in KYC/AML processes and identity verification
- **Governance**: Purpose-built for regulated markets with on-chain governance
- **Confidentiality**: Granular control over transaction and asset data visibility
- **Settlement Finality**: Deterministic settlement with built-in mechanisms for corporate actions
- **Asset Servicing**: Native support for complex financial instruments and corporate actions
